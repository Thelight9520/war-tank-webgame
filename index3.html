WarTanks Game Guide

Player One (Red Tank)

Controls

W Move forward
S Move backward
A Turn left
D Turn right
SPACE Fire bullet

Player Two (Blue Tank)

Controls

↑ Move forward
↓ Move backward
← Turn left
→ Turn right
ENTER Fire bullet

Control Scheme

Player 1

Movement: WASD

Shoot: SPACEBAR

Tank: Red

Starts: Left Side

Special function when going backwards/

Player 2

Movement: Arrow Keys

Shoot: ENTER

Tank: Blue

Starts: Right Side

Special function when going backwards
